Our History

Carat Live began as a bold vision to redefine how fans connect with their favorite artists. What started as a simple idea to make concert tickets more accessible quickly grew into a platform that has brought millions of fans closer to the artists they love. In the early days, it wasn’t just about selling tickets — it was about crafting an experience that made every fan feel like a VIP.

Our breakthrough came when we partnered with some of the biggest names in the music industry, including global K-pop superstars Seventeen and Blackpink. These collaborations catapulted Carat Live into the spotlight, and fans from all over the world trusted us to deliver the best seats for sold-out shows. As our platform expanded, we embraced diverse genres, adding LANY's indie-pop magic and Brunos Mars's dazzling performances and the iconic Coldplay, whose immersive and electrifying live shows unite fans from every corner of the world. From the beginning, the focus has been on providing a reliable, fast, and user-friendly platform to help music lovers get closer to their favorite artists with just a few clicks. At Carat Live, we're proud to handle ticketing for some of the most iconic artists in the world.

But what truly set Carat Live apart was our commitment to innovation. We introduced cutting-edge technologies that made ticket purchasing smoother and faster. Fans no longer had to worry about missing out on tickets, and we created personalized experiences that let users feel connected to their favorite artists beyond just the concert.

Today, Carat Live stands as more than just a ticketing service; it's a gateway to unforgettable memories, a platform where every fan’s journey to their dream concert begins. With each event we handle, we’re not just selling tickets — we’re building a legacy of incredible live music experiences.
